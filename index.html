Frequently asked questions

Self is a financial technology company that helps people build credit and savings at the same time. Our flagship product, the Credit Builder Account, is a small loan held in a CD. As you make monthly payments, they are reported to the three major credit bureaus, helping you build a positive payment history.

Self reports your on-time monthly payments to all three major credit bureaus — Experian, Equifax, and TransUnion. Payment history is the most important factor in your credit score (35%).

If you're starting your credit journey or rebuilding damaged credit, the Credit Builder Account is the best starting point — no credit check and reports to all 3 bureaus.

Members see an average credit score increase of 47 points* with a Credit Builder Account.
